Centre for Science and Environment (CSE)

Vision

At CSE, their vision is of a world where development is not pursued at the expense of the environment but is harmoniously aligned with nature's limits. They envision thriving ecosystems, clean air and water, and resilient communities living in harmony with the planet.

Mission

CSE's mission revolves around fostering sustainable and equitable development while safeguarding the environment. Their primary goal is to empower communities and policymakers with knowledge and solutions to address pressing environmental challenges and promote a greener and healthier planet.

Projects

1.Environmental Research: Conducting cutting-edge research and analysis to provide evidence-based insights on environmental issues and sustainable development.
2.Policy Advocacy: Engaging with policymakers and advocating for progressive policies that promote sustainable practices and protect the environment.
3.Capacity Building: Empowering individuals and communities through knowledge-sharing and capacity-building initiatives, enabling them to be active participants in environmental conservation.
