Wildlife Trust of India (WTI)

Vision

At WTI, the vision is of a country where wildlife thrives in its natural habitat, and human activities coexist in harmony with nature. They envision a landscape where ecosystems are healthy, diverse, and resilient, ensuring the survival of wildlife species and ecological balance.

Mission

WTI's mission centers around preserving India's unique wildlife heritage for future generations. They strive to protect endangered species, conserve critical habitats, and promote sustainable practices that harmonize the needs of wildlife and local communities.

Projects

Species Conservation: Implementing focused conservation programs for endangered species, such as tigers, elephants, rhinoceros, and various other threatened wildlife.
Habitat Conservation: Undertaking measures to protect and restore critical habitats, ensuring a secure environment for wildlife to thrive.
Anti-Poaching Efforts: Collaborating with law enforcement agencies to combat wildlife poaching and illegal trade of wildlife products.
