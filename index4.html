The Energy and Resources Institute (TERI)

Vision

At TERI, the vision is of a world where societies flourish in harmony with nature, relying on clean energy sources, and ensuring ecological balance. They envision a future where economic growth and environmental stewardship go hand in hand, ensuring the well-being of current and future generations.

Mission

TERI's mission revolves around fostering a sustainable future through cutting-edge research, policy advocacy, and capacity-building programs. They are committed to addressing environmental challenges, promoting renewable energy, and advancing sustainable development practices.

Projects

Climate Change and Adaptation: Conducting research on climate change impacts, vulnerabilities, and adaptation strategies to support climate-resilient communities and ecosystems.
Renewable Energy: Promoting renewable energy technologies and advocating for their adoption to reduce carbon emissions and combat climate change.
Environmental Policy and Governance: Engaging with policymakers and contributing to the development of progressive environmental policies and regulations.
