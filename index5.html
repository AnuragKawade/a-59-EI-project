The Nature Conservancy (TNC) India

Vision

At TNC India, the vision is of a world where thriving ecosystems support biodiversity, clean air, water, and a resilient environment. They envision a future where nature is safeguarded, and communities live in harmony with the natural world, deriving benefits from its abundance.

Mission

TNC India's mission revolves around protecting and restoring India's diverse ecosystems and natural resources. They are dedicated to finding innovative solutions that balance the needs of both nature and people, ensuring a sustainable future for all.

Projects

Habitat Conservation: Implementing projects to safeguard critical habitats, such as forests, wetlands, grasslands, and marine areas, for the protection of biodiversity and ecological balance.
Climate Resilience: Addressing climate change impacts through research, adaptation strategies, and community-based resilience-building measures.
Sustainable Agriculture: Promoting sustainable agricultural practices that enhance productivity while preserving natural resources and biodiversity.
